Privacy Policy for SudokuZen

Last Updated: December 2, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how SudokuZen ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

SudokuZen is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary for the app to function:

  • Game Progress Data: Puzzle completion status, solve times, statistics, and game state (stored locally on your device)
  • Settings Preferences: Your app settings such as sound preferences, theme selections, and notification preferences (stored locally on your device)
  • Achievement Data: Your unlocked achievements and progress (stored locally on your device)
  • Statistics: Game statistics including puzzles completed, streaks, and XP earned (stored locally on your device)

Important: We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes

2. No User Accounts Required

SudokuZen does not require you to create an account or sign in. All data is stored locally on your device. This means:

  • No cloud sync or backup (unless you use Android's built-in backup feature)
  • No user authentication or login required
  • No personal information associated with your game data

3. How We Use Your Information

All data collected is used solely for:

  • Providing game functionality and features
  • Maintaining your game progress and statistics
  • Personalizing your app experience (themes, settings)
  • Displaying achievements and tracking your progress

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain information as described in Google's Privacy Policy. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (for ad personalization)
  • Advertising ID
  • App usage data related to ads

For more information about how Google uses data when you use our app, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

5. Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device using:

  • Room Database: For puzzle data and game state
  • DataStore: For settings, preferences, and user progress

Your data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. If you uninstall the app, all local data will be deleted.

6. Android Backup

Android's automatic backup feature may back up your app data to your Google account. This is controlled by Android system settings, not by our app. You can manage this in your device's backup settings.

7. Children's Privacy

SudokuZen is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we do not require accounts or collect personal information, this is not a concern with our current data practices.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: All your data is stored locally on your device
  • Delete your data: Uninstall the app or use the "Reset" options in Settings
  • Opt out of ads: Use your device settings to opt out of personalized ads

To delete all app data:

  • Uninstall the app from your device, or
  • Use the "Reset Statistics" and "Reset Achievements" options in the app Settings

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
